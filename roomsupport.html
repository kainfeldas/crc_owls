Room Support

Our team can check, maintain, and troubleshoot A/V issues in supported rooms, as well as provide pre-meeting support.

Room Support Services

Stanford's University IT (UIT) offers various room support services with different rates depending on the type of support and client status. Room Event Support, for example, can be hourly or monthly.

Room Event Support

  • On-site support: $97.75 per hour for clients with a Computer Resource Consulting (CRC) contract; $122 per hour for those without.
  • Remote support (phone hotline, routine sweeps, documentation, 3 incidents/month): $47.70 per room, per month.
  • Incidents beyond the included number (equipment under warranty): $31.20 per incident.

Hardware and Software A/V Support

  • Tier 2-4 support: $552 per year / $46 per month.
  • Time & material support (without AVT agreement, 2-hour minimum): $175 per hour.
  • Time & material support (with AVT agreement, 2-hour minimum): $125 per hour.
  • Provisioning Cisco codecs: $525 per codec registration.

For more detailed information, including other support options and rates, visit the Stanford University's UIT website.
