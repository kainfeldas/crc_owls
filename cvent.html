Cvent Service Overview

Stanford University IT offers Cvent, a comprehensive event management platform designed to streamline the planning and execution of events. Cvent provides tools for event registration, attendee management, event marketing, and reporting. The platform is suitable for various types of events, including conferences, seminars, workshops, and virtual events.

Key Features:

  • Event Registration: Create customized registration forms and manage attendee information.
  • Event Marketing: Promote events through email campaigns and social media integration.
  • Attendee Management: Track attendee participation and engagement.
  • Reporting and Analytics: Generate detailed reports on event performance and attendee data.
  • Virtual Events: Host webinars and virtual meetings with integrated video conferencing tools.

Benefits:

  • Efficiency: Automate event management tasks to save time and reduce manual effort.
  • Customization: Tailor event registration and marketing to meet specific needs.
  • Data Insights: Gain valuable insights into event performance and attendee behavior.

Support:

Stanford University IT provides support for Cvent users, including training and troubleshooting assistance.
